ABOUT

Emile K. is a young creative prodigy, part of the new Antwerp golden generation. A scholar at AP Hogeschool, he is an expert at InDesign and skillfull master at Adobe Photoshop. As a designer, his mother tongue is JavaScript, while also fluent at html and css.
